Partner Model

The Upgrade Is Earned.

The Partner model is not automatic. To be considered, a Strategist must demonstrate two qualifications:

Qualification 01

Proven Ability to Sell

A track record of independently sourcing and closing clients.

Qualification 02

Client Retention

A minimum of 6 months managing clients with strong retention (benchmarks TBD).

⚑ There is no fixed MRR threshold for the Partner upgrade — it is merit-based. Commission begins at $10K/month MRR under management.

What's Expected

Full-Time. In-Person. All In.

This is a full-time, in-person commitment during all business hours. In the early stages you will shadow Kyle on sales and Tyler on fulfillment to build the foundational skills required to succeed at both.

Early Stage Focus

Sales First — By Design.

The majority of early time is weighted toward sales. You cannot manage revenue you have not yet acquired. Building your MRR base quickly is the priority — and the fastest path to unlocking the full income potential of either track.
