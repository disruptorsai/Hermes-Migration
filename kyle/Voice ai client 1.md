## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Are you licensed and insured?

We’re fully insured for exterior cleaning work. In our area, a license isn’t required for this industry, but we still operate with proper coverage so customers can feel confident moving forward.

### Do you offer free estimates?

Yes, we provide free estimates for exterior cleaning projects. We recommend reaching out by phone or form so we can start the estimate process right away.

### Do you give prices online or offer “package pricing” on the website?

No two exteriors are exactly alike. That’s why we visit every location to provide pricing that reflects the real conditions and the full scope of work.

### Do I need to be home during the exterior cleaning service?

Not at all, you don’t have to be home for us to complete the service. We ask that you ensure your water is on, windows and doors are closed, gates are unlocked, and any payment arrangements are handled in advance, then our team can take care of the rest.

### What are your business hours and weekend availability?

Our standard hours are Monday–Friday, 8am–5pm, and weekends are by appointment only.

### How do you communicate with customers before, during, and after service?

We communicate in whatever way is most convenient for the customer, and most updates are handled by text message when possible. We can also use phone calls and email. Our team keeps communication flexible, so you’re never guessing what’s happening.

### Do you use pressure washing or soft washing?

We’re a soft wash company because high pressure can cause irreparable damage to most exterior surfaces. Our team focuses on using the right solution and low pressure rather than relying on “more PSI” to get results.

### Will your exterior cleaning damage my home, siding, or roof?

No, when done correctly, exterior cleaning should never damage your home. That’s why we don’t rely on high pressure. We utilize the correct method and solution for each surface rather than turning up the pressure and risking issues such as surface wear or damage.

### Are your cleaning solutions environmentally friendly and safe for the property?

We describe our process and solutions as environmentally and property-friendly when used correctly. Our team follows strict procedures for proper site preparation, accurate dilution ratios, and controlled, low-pressure application so the cleaning stays effective while protecting the home and surrounding areas.

### How do you protect landscaping, plants, and delicate exterior areas?

We take active protection steps, including covering plants and shrubbery with breathable material when needed. We also protect sensitive areas like doors, doorbells, cameras, and other delicate surfaces as part of our setup process.

### Do you bring your own water for exterior cleaning jobs?

Although our trucks carry onboard water tanks to regulate flow and pressure, we must connect to the customer’s water source to refill the tank throughout the service. This ensures consistent performance while protecting the home’s plumbing system from being overdrawn.

If water access is not available, service may still be possible using our onboard supply only; however, additional charges will apply due to increased time, fuel usage, and logistical requirements.

### What happens if the weather is bad on the day of service?

Bad weather usually postpones the job rather than “preventing” it. We take conditions like heavy rain, high winds, and extreme temperatures seriously because they affect safety and the ability to protect your property properly.

### How do you manage safety and regulations for commercial services?

We take compliance seriously based on the site and scope. Our team plans around things like permits, drainage/runoff protection, and on-site safety controls, and we coordinate closely with the property contact or manager.

### Do you offer any discounts?

Yes! Customers can receive a 3% cash or check discount, and additional discounts (including military or senior) may be available upon request. We also offer bundle discounts when two or more full-priced services are scheduled together.
