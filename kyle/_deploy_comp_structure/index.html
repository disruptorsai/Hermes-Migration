Guaranteed base pay plus tiered portfolio bonuses and self-sourced client overrides. Designed for consistent growth.

Track Two

Tier 2

Self-Sourced Client Override.

A separate, higher-paying structure that rewards Strategists who bring their own clients into the agency.

Override Rate
10%
Of gross revenue on any client you personally source and close
How It Works
  • Stacks on top of base and any Tier 1 bonus you qualify for
  • Applies to every self-sourced client in your portfolio
  • A $20K/mo self-sourced client = $24,000/year override alone
⚑ Self-sourced overrides are in addition to your base pay and Tier 1 managed bonuses. They compound as your personal book grows.

Advancement

Path to Partner.

Graduation to Partner is on the table for any Strategist who proves themselves over time. Two requirements must be met:

Requirement 01

Sustained Client Retention

Demonstrate satisfactory performance and client retention over an extended period — typically 1 to 2 years.

Requirement 02

Independent Sales Ability

Source and close your own clients multiple times with no sales assistance from leadership or anyone else on the team.

⚑ Partner graduation is a deliberate decision, not an automatic threshold. Both requirements must be demonstrated before consideration.
