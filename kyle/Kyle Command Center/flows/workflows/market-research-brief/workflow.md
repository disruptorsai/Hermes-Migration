# Market Research Brief

## Overview
A 4-step automated research workflow that takes a niche or topic as input and produces a comprehensive market research brief.

## Steps
1. **Pick Niche** - Define and validate the target market
2. **Find Competitors** - Web research to identify key players
3. **Analyze Positioning** - Deep-dive into competitor strategies
4. **Write Report** - Compile everything into a structured brief

## Input
- A niche, market, or topic to research (e.g., "AI video editing tools", "premium dog food brands")

## Output
- A markdown report covering: market overview, competitor profiles, positioning analysis, gaps and opportunities
