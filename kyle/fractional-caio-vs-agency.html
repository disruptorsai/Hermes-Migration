They Rent Their Systems To You

Long-Term Contracts

Questions You're Probably Asking

The fair questions, answered straight.

These come up in every conversation. Here's exactly how the model works — and where the risk actually lives.

Question 01
We already have a marketing agency.
Straight Answer
Ask one question: what do you own when they leave? If the answer is "nothing," they're not your partner — they're your landlord. We build inside the stack you already own, so the leverage compounds whether we stay six months or six years.
Question 02
Isn't this just expensive consulting?
Straight Answer
Consultants recommend. We build, ship, and train — live, inside your tools. By month three, you have working AI in production and a team that can operate it. That's a fraction of what an agency costs and a fraction of what a full-time AI hire costs.
Question 03
My team isn't technical enough for this.
Straight Answer
That's exactly the point. We teach AI as we install it. Every working session leaves your team smarter and the system more operable. The goal is for you to not need us — faster than any agency would ever let you.
Question 04
Why month-to-month? Doesn't that feel risky?
Straight Answer
It's the opposite. We have to earn the renewal every 30 days. Long contracts protect agencies from being fired. Month-to-month protects you from a partner who stops shipping. The risk lives on our side — by design.
