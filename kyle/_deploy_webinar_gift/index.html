How It Gets Built

Five layers, one autonomous system.

01
Intake Layer

A trained AI voice agent (Retell, Vapi, or similar) paired with an SMS autoresponder captures every lead across every channel — web forms, missed calls, Facebook, Google LSAs, live chat.

02
Qualification Intelligence

The agent follows a conversation script trained on your business. It asks the same questions your best salesperson would. It qualifies out tire-kickers and flags the hot ones.

03
Real-Time Routing

Hot leads get instantly routed to the owner or sales rep by SMS, call, or Slack ping. Cold leads get nurtured automatically.

04
Calendar Booking

The agent books directly on your calendar, confirms by text and email, and sends pre-appointment reminders to reduce no-shows.

05
CRM Sync

Every interaction, transcript, and outcome flows into your CRM (GoHighLevel, HubSpot, or similar), so nothing falls through the cracks and you have a full paper trail.

How It Gets Built

Four phases, one closed loop.

01
Competitive Intelligence & Ad Reverse Engineering

AI scrapes the Meta Ad Library, TikTok Creative Center, Google's Ads Transparency Center, and tools like Foreplay to pull every high-performing ad in your niche. It analyzes hooks, visuals, angles, CTAs — and builds a database of what's actually working, removing the guesswork that kills most campaigns before they start.

02
On-Brand Creative Generation At Scale

Full access to your library of brand photos, videos, testimonials, and raw footage. From there, the AI generates new on-brand images automatically (static ads, UGC-style graphics, before/after) via Higgsfield, Nano Banana, Midjourney. Video stays human-led but AI-accelerated — a human editor paired with AI can produce 5 variations of a winning video in the time a traditional editor makes 1.

03
Autonomous Campaign Management

Plugs into Meta Ads Manager (Google, TikTok, LinkedIn) and runs continuously. Watches fatigue signals, auto-pauses creative that's burning money, scales winners within the hour instead of the week, and launches new variations against top performers to keep the flywheel spinning.

04
Closed-Loop With System 01

Ads that generate inbound leads hand off directly to your AI Lead Response Agent. Interested prospects get replies within 60 seconds while they're still in the buying window. No lead goes cold. No budget gets wasted.
