# Analyze Positioning

## Purpose
Evaluate each competitor's market positioning, pricing strategy, and unique value propositions.

## Input
- Competitor list from the Find Competitors step

## Process
1. Visit each competitor's website and key pages
2. Extract pricing tiers, feature lists, and messaging
3. Identify each competitor's unique selling points
4. Map competitors on a positioning grid (price vs. feature depth)

## Output
- Per-competitor profile with pricing, positioning, strengths, and weaknesses
