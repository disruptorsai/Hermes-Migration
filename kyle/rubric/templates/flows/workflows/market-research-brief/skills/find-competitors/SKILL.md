# Find Competitors

## Purpose
Search the web to identify the key players and competitors operating in the target niche.

## Input
- Niche definition from the Pick Niche step

## Process
1. Run web searches for the niche keywords and related terms
2. Identify companies, products, and services in the space
3. Filter for relevance and market presence
4. Collect basic info: name, URL, tagline, apparent size

## Output
- List of 5-15 competitors with names, URLs, and brief descriptions
