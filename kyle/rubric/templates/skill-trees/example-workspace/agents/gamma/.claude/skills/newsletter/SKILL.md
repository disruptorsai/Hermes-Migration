# Newsletter

When writing a newsletter:

1. Subject line: curiosity-driven, under 50 characters
2. Opening line: personal, conversational — like texting a friend
3. Body: one main idea, scannable with bold and bullets
4. CTA: one clear action, not three
5. Keep total length under 500 words
