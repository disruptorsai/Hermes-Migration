## Brainstorm Workflow

This workflow triggers the **Superpowers Brainstorming Skill** for Socratic design refinement.

### Steps

1. Read the brainstorming skill file at `.agent/skills/superpowers/skills/brainstorming/SKILL.md`
2. Follow the skill instructions **exactly as presented** — this includes a Socratic Q&A process to explore the user's requirements and design before any implementation begins.
3. The goal is to tease out a clear specification through conversation, showing chunks short enough to actually read and digest.
4. Do NOT jump into implementation. The brainstorm must be completed and approved first.
