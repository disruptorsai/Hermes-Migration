Purpose of this document — an internal artifact for Dillon to align the founder and tech lead (Nate) around a concrete path forward as the current BD role is transitioned out. It outlines the inefficiencies identified in our conversation, what a Disruptors Media partnership looks like, and the AI systems we'd build — prioritized by ROI.

Where Revenue Leaks

Inefficiencies We Heard.

Solestiss closes almost everything that reaches proposal — the leaks are upstream of that, in the spaces between busy executives, warm intros, and a CRM that doesn't exist yet.

01

No Central CRM

Pipeline spread across spreadsheets and 20 people's memory. No single source of truth for leads, stages, or re-engagement history.

02

Manual Follow-Up

Reminders run through Gemini and personal discipline. Warm intros cool off while busy execs are chased one-off.

03

No Appointment Automation

Zero systematic pre-meeting reminders by email or SMS. Show rates depend entirely on who remembers to ping whom.

04

Pre-Call Conviction Gap

Five to six vertical decks exist but are only shown live. Prospects arrive cold — no pre-delivered proof of what Solestiss does.

05

Ad-Hoc LinkedIn Cadence

LinkedIn is the #1 authority channel — and the source of cold inbound — but posting is impulse- and news-cycle-driven, not systematic.

06

Inbound Triage Drain

LinkedIn DMs mix qualified buyers with startup dreamers. Manual filtering consumes senior time that should be on client-facing BD.

07

Unsystematic Onboarding

New clients go straight to work inside client systems — no standardized agreement, payment, or kickoff flow to accelerate activation.

08

Underperforming BD Seat

Three senior consultants close 99% of business. The BD lead has closed zero — paying premium for no pipeline lift.

The Build

AI Systems We'd Develop.

Sequenced by ROI. The first three address the highest-leverage gap — turning warm intros and proposals into closed revenue before they cool.

01

GoHighLevel CRM Foundation

Single source of truth. Separate pipelines per vertical (utility, OEM, sub-supplier, state gov, new-entrant). Replaces spreadsheets and personal memory. Starts at ~$90/mo — inexpensive, foundational, unlocks every system below.

Foundation
02

Automated Follow-Up & Reminder Sequences

Email + SMS drip before, after, and between meetings. Warm intros never go cold. Triggered by pipeline stage — not by whether Dillon remembers. Highest-impact hour of implementation in the entire build.

Highest ROI
03

Vertical-Matched Pre-Meeting Video Delivery

Your five to six existing decks converted into short pre-call videos, auto-sent based on the lead's vertical. Prospects arrive warmed up with proof — lifts show rate and shortens the sell inside the call.

Conversion Lift
04

LinkedIn Content Operating System

Editorial cadence + news-cycle-responsive rapid-post workflow for Dillon and the founder. Keeps the authority channel disciplined without losing the spontaneity that drives engagement today.

Authority
05

LinkedIn Inbox Triage Agent

AI pre-sorts inbound DMs — qualified buyer vs. startup dreamer vs. sweat-equity pitch — so senior consultants only see what's worth a reply. Reclaims hours off the "deal with the crazies" inbox.

Time Recovery
06

Referral Capture & Nurture

Text, email, and LinkedIn warm intros logged into the CRM automatically with source attribution. Nurture cadence keeps second-degree network paths alive between opportunities.

Network Loop
07

Onboarding Automation

Agreement generation, payment collection, kickoff document intake — standardized but flexible enough to respect each client's confidentiality and cyber rules.

Activation
08

AI Consultant Personas (Scaled Internal Use)

Systematize what Dillon already does ad-hoc — tone-matched drafters, devil's-advocate reviewers, vertical-specific analysts — shared across the team so every consultant gets the same leverage.

Team Leverage
09

AI-Search Visibility (ChatGPT / Perplexity)

Position Solestiss's thought pieces to surface when C-suite and policy audiences research nuclear strategy inside AI tools. Lower priority — your warm network drives revenue — but cheap authority insurance.
